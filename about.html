About Us

Egemen Tours LLC was founded in 2019 as a company for providing service in aviation and tourism sector. On that year Egemen Tours became General Sales Agent of Pegasus Airlines in Kyrgyz Republic.


Over the years, it has developed many specific services for airlines, tour operators, exporters and shippers.

Nowadays Egemen Tours provides such kind of services as airline representation GSA, supervision, sales, consultancy in aviation sector, ground handling, organization of tours, cargo shipping, transfer, fueling etc. Company has two offices in city centers of Bishkek and Osh.


Egemen Tours has agreement with more than 200 local tour operators and ticket agencies.


By offering you our services we also offer you our experiences in the aviation sector. We can meet all needs of customers on the ground as well as in the air.

Egemen Tours is giving consultancy services to both local and international airlines and companies. Egemen Tours provides top quality services, with the flexibility of 24/7 operations and immediate response to any business or personal service requests.

Mission And Vision

Our vision as Egemen Tours is to render our brand as a secure, preferable and well-known company in aviation sector of Kyrgyzstan. Our high caliber staff is dedicated to presenting exceptional services to passengers by providing with creative solutions tailored to their needs.


Egemen Tours supports the initiative of the progressive circles of the civil aviation sector of Kyrgyz Republic, which united around a common goal - to improve aviation sector.
